Numbers as Bits

Numbers are used as a form of bits too for the display of information. A group of eight bits makes a byte. As for the binary number system, only two numbers are used: "0" means an off-computer, while "1" means on-computer.

Compression of Pictures

With lossless data compression, there is always the ability to use fewer bits while ensuring that all the original data is still present. In the case of lossy compression, the bits are reduced to a great extent, but only some recessed forms of data are retained. Compression helps reduce the time taken to transmit information over the browser and saves more space.

Data ETL

Managing data is not easy. Often, it may be disorganized, incomplete, or incorrect, which means you may have to consolidate data from multiple sources.

Using Data Related Programs

Programs are used to process data, enabling the users to gain knowledge. Programs can also be used to clean and filter digital data.
